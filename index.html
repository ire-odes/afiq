Get started

Installation in 8 steps

01

Download the installer

Click the Download for Windows button above and save AirFlowIQ_Setup.exe to your desktop.

02

Run the installer

Go to your desktop and double-click AirFlowIQ_Setup.exe to launch the setup wizard.

03

Bypass the Windows security prompt

Windows may show a blue "Windows protected your PC" screen. Click More info, then click Run anyway. This is normal for new apps.

⚠️ This warning appears because the app isn't yet signed with a Microsoft certificate — it is completely safe to proceed.
04

Create an account and sign in

Once the app opens, click Create Account on the login screen, enter your email and a password, then sign in.

05

Open the Devices tab

Click the ☰ hamburger menu in the top-left corner, then select Devices from the sidebar.

06

Click "Find Device"

In the top-right corner of the Devices tab, click the green ⌕ Find Device button.

07

Enter your device's MAC address

Type in the MAC address printed on the label on the bottom of your AirFlow IQ device. It looks like: 1A:BC:D2:3B:3E:4F

08

Search and claim your device

Click Search. When your device appears, click Yes to add it to your account. Your sensor data will begin loading on the Dashboard immediately.
