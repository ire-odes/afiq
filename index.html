AIRFLOW IQ APP INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Download the application using the button above and save it to your desktop.
  2. Go to your desktop and double click AirFlowIQ.exe.
  3. When prompted, click “Read more” then click “Run anyway”.
  4. On the login page, create an account and sign in.
  5. Navigate to the Devices tab on the left.
  6. In the top right corner of the Devices tab, click “Find Device”.
  7. In the field that appears, type in the MAC address that is printed on the case of your device.
  8. Click Search, then click Add.
